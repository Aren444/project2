Site Map

Organization and Features

  1. Navigation Menu –
    An always accessible hamburger menu allowing users to jump to sections without scrolling (Pages include Overview, Gallery/Tour, Pricing, Inspection Report, Other info)


  2. High-Impact Visuals –
    A large photo of our home on the homepage, and quality pics in gallery.


  3. QR Code Integration –
    Flyers will include a QR code that links to the website.


  4. Mobile Optimization –
    Ensure the website is responsive for a seamless experience on smartphones and tablets.


  5. Virtual Tour Option –
    A video walkthrough tour to offer buyers an immersive experience.


  6. Call-to-Action (CTA) –
    Clear CTAs on each page including "Contact to Place a Bid" and "Schedule a Viewing".


  7. Social Sharing –
    Easy social media sharing to help spread the listing, maybe another CTA button?


  8. FAQ Section –
    A dedicated section answering common buyer questions, such as property taxes, utility costs, HOA fees.

Map Integration

• Interactive Google Map –
Shows my house location with an embedded Google Map, users can explore nearby amenities.

• Neighborhood Highlights –
Use Google My Maps to highlight key locations such as schools, parks, shopping centers, and major roads.
