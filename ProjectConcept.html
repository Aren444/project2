Project Concept

Target Market

Homebuyers in the Calvert County area, particularly families looking for good schools and those comfortable with a 40 minute commute to Andrews Air Force base or a less than one-hour commute to Washington, D.C.


Website Goals

1. Engagement –
Create an intuitive and visually appealing platform that allows potential buyers to explore the home easily.

2. Information –
Provide clear, detailed sections covering price, photos, neighborhood insights, tax details, inspection reports, local data showing school ratings, crime rates, average home values.

3. Direct Contact –
Offer a method for interested buyers to reach out and submit a bid without going through a realtor, reducing costs for both parties.
